Welcome to Wolfpack '24.

This is our homebase. It's a centralized collection of all the events and initiatives that are unique to the McMaster Bachelor of Health Sciences (Honours) Class of 2024. That includes academic resources for our courses, upcoming events, and promoting class-wide spirit. All the content on this website is managed by the Bachelor of Health Sciences Society First Year Council.

Upcoming Exams!

There are many exams coming up for members of our cohort, so our Academic Team has organized several events!

Cell Bio

We are once again putting together a collaborative document where everyone may share their hypotheses on practice NOCAT problems and view the ideas that others came up with. To access this document, please submit a hypothesis here and the document will be shared with you shortly after. We have also updated our NOCAT problem-solving guide document to include relevant new content from this semester. This document can be found here.

Psych

We have created Anki cards for all of the content leading up to the Quizterm! These can be found in the PSYCH 1XX3 sub-folder of our academic resources folder, which can also be accessed through this link.

Medterm

We have created a cheatsheet/review guide containing all of the terms learned so far, as well as their combining forms, adjectival forms, and Latin forms. This can be found here. We have also made a note compiling some of them in a more accessible place here. And finally, since we could all use a little bit of productive fun, the Academic Team will be hosting a Medterm Codenames game on Sunday, February 21st, at 8:00 pm. The link to join is here, and all participants and winners will be awarded house points!
